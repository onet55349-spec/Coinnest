Platform Quick Start Guide

1

Create Account & Verify

Sign up with your email and proceed to the **Verification** page. You must upload a clear image of your Identity Document for security compliance. Trading is restricted until an administrator approves your verification.

2

Fund Your Wallet

Go to the **Wallet** section and copy your unique USDT (BEP20) deposit address. Transfer funds from your external wallet. Once sent, contact **Customer Care** with your txn hash for instant credit.

3

Start Trading

Navigate to the **Trade** tab. Select your asset (BTC, ETH, etc.) and choose a direction (Buy/Sell). If you have been granted a "Win Signal" by the platform administrator, your outcomes will be optimized for success.

Security & Withdrawal Rules

Manual Approval Required

To prevent unauthorized access, every withdrawal from CoinNest requires manual authorization. After submitting a withdrawal request in the wallet, you **must** message our Customer Care team in the support chat to finalize the payout.

2FA and Account Safety

Never share your password or referral code with anyone. Official CoinNest admins will never ask for your private keys. Always check the "Account Status" indicator in your profile to ensure your sessions are secure.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does verification take?

Admins typically review KYC documents within 2-6 hours. You will receive a notification once approved.

What is a "Win Signal"?

The Win Signal is a specialized indicator granted to certain accounts by the administrator, ensuring a higher probability of success during active sessions.
